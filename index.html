Welcome to Rasnov

Explore Historic Fortress, Stunning Nature & Romanian Culture

Top Locations to Visit

Rasnov Fortress

A medieval citadel built by Teutonic Knights in the 13th century. Offers breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding Carpathian Mountains.

2-3 hours 20 RON

Dino Parc

The largest dinosaur park in Southeast Europe with life-size animatronic dinosaurs. Perfect for families and children.

1-2 hours 40 RON

Piatra Mica Peak

Hiking trail to a stunning mountain peak. Accessible via cable car or hiking trail, offering spectacular mountain views.

3-4 hours Free (30 RON cable car)

Village Museum

Explore traditional Romanian rural life with authentic houses, tools, and artifacts from the region's history.

1 hour 10 RON
