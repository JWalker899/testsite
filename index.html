Welcome to Rasnov

Explore Historic Fortress, Stunning Nature & Romanian Culture

Top Locations to Visit

Rasnov Fortress

A medieval citadel built by Teutonic Knights in the 13th century. Offers breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding Carpathian Mountains.

2-3 hours 20 RON

Dino Parc

The largest dinosaur park in Southeast Europe with life-size animatronic dinosaurs. Perfect for families and children.

1-2 hours 40 RON

Piatra Mica Peak

Hiking trail to a stunning mountain peak. Accessible via cable car or hiking trail, offering spectacular mountain views.

3-4 hours Free (30 RON cable car)

Village Museum

Explore traditional Romanian rural life with authentic houses, tools, and artifacts from the region's history.

1 hour 10 RON

Bran Castle

Famous Dracula's Castle, just 15 minutes away. Gothic fortress with fascinating history and stunning architecture.

2 hours 45 RON

Poiana Brasov Ski Resort

Premier ski resort nearby with 23km of slopes. Great for winter sports enthusiasts and summer hiking.

Full day 150 RON/day

Brasov Old Town

Medieval city center with Council Square, Black Church, and charming cobblestone streets. Only 15km away.

3-4 hours Free

Peles Castle

Neo-Renaissance masterpiece in Sinaia. One of Europe's most beautiful castles with 160 lavishly decorated rooms.

2-3 hours 50 RON

Piatra Craiului National Park

Protected natural area with dramatic limestone ridge. Excellent hiking, wildlife watching, and pristine nature.

Full day Free

Libearty Bear Sanctuary

Europe's largest brown bear sanctuary. Home to rescued bears in natural habitat. Educational and ethical tourism.

1-2 hours 25 RON
